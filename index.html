BEAUTY'S BEHOLDERS EXTENDED

This is a project on a block in North Beach neighborhood, San Francisco, California. It shows the discovery of beauty through forms, textures, and colors of plants. Beauty can be discovered and interpreted differently between each person according to their own understanding, imagination, and perception. So, feel free to create things and explore your idea of beauty here.
